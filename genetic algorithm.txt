1. Genetic Algorithm (GA)

Description:
A Genetic Algorithm is an optimization technique inspired by natural selection. It solves problems by generating a population of possible solutions and improving them through selection, crossover, and mutation over multiple generations.

Key idea: Survival of the fittest.

Examples:

1. Feature Selection in Machine Learning
GA selects the best subset of features to improve model accuracy (e.g., for your Rice Leaf Disease Detection project).
2. Scheduling Problems
Used to optimize class schedules or job assignments to minimize conflicts and maximize efficiency.
